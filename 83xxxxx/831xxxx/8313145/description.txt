This is the 3rd of Tony Bertot's Assassin Trilogy. The Legacy of the Assassin picks up where The Heart of an Assassin left off.

Follow Tyler Santiago as he starts to unravel the mystery surrounding his father while at the same time Felicia Giordano puts a contract out on the assassin and anyone that may be connected to him.

You won't be able to put the book down as the characters come to life before your very eyes and you find yourself rooting for the bad guys.
