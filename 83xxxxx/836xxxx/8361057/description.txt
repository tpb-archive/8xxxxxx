Also known as Out Of This World, Another World is a pioneer action/platformer

hurtled through space and time by a nuclear experiment that goes wrong. In an

the host of alien monsters, while surviving an environment as deadly as your
enemies. Only a perfect blend of logic and skill will get you past the deadly
obstacles that lie in wait.
